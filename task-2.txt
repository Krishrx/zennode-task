1. Why do you like to join as a Software developer?
    Though I'm from circuit branches, Becoming a software developer is exciting for me because it's like being a digital architect. I get to build things from scratch, solve puzzles, and bring ideas to life and get satisfaction in seeing my code turn into functional applications.

2. List top 3 softwares / tools that inspire you. Why do these tools inspire you?
    Visual Studio Code (VSCode): I love the simplicity and efficiency of VSCode. It's feature-rich, customizable, and has a vibrant extension ecosystem. This tool makes coding enjoyable.

    React.js: React.js remains a key inspiration for its declarative and efficient way of building UI components. It promotes a modular and reusable approach, enhancing the overall development experience.

    Tailwind CSS: I'm inspired by how Tailwind streamlines the styling process and allows for rapid development without sacrificing flexibility. Its utility-first approach and simplicity in styling components bring a new level of efficiency to CSS.

3. List top 3 organisations you like to join in your career & why you like these organisations.
    Google: Google is a dream for many developers and I'm one among them. I'm drawn to Google because of its innovative culture and the impactful projects they undertake. The emphasis on creativity, problem-solving, and the potential to work on cutting-edge technologies is a big pull for me.

    Instagram: The dynamic environment and focus on visual storytelling at Instagram resonate with my passion for creating engaging user experiences. Joining a platform that has such a significant impact on how people connect through visuals would be incredibly fulfilling.

    Spotify: I just love UI & UX of spotify. Being part of a company that revolutionizes how we consume and discover music aligns perfectly with my love for tech and creativity.
